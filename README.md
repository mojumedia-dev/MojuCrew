# MojuCrew

**AI Staff on Demand for Small & Medium Businesses**

MojuCrew is a modular AI SaaS platform that gives SMBs access to a team of AI workers — each filling a real business role like receptionist, CRM manager, review responder, and market researcher — all under one dashboard.

> Feels like hiring. Priced like a subscription.

---

## The Problem

SMBs need help with customer communication, lead follow-up, online reputation, and market awareness — but they can't afford full-time staff for every role. Existing tools are either too complex, too expensive, or only solve one piece of the puzzle.

## The Solution

MojuCrew provides plug-and-play AI bots, each designed to fill a specific business role. No technical setup. No enterprise contracts. Just pick your crew and go.

---

## Core Modules

| Bot | Role |
|-----|------|
| **MojuChat** | Live chat receptionist — answers questions, books appointments, qualifies leads |
| **MojuCRM** | CRM manager — logs interactions, follows up with leads, manages pipeline |
| **MojuReviews** | Review manager — monitors & responds to Google/Yelp reviews, requests new ones |
| **MojuResearch** | Market researcher — tracks competitors, summarizes trends, delivers weekly insights |
| **MojuContent** | Social & content — drafts posts, repurposes content, engages comments |

---

## Initial Target Markets (MVP)

Avoiding PII-heavy industries initially. Focus on:

- Retail shops
- Restaurants & cafes
- Trades (plumbers, electricians, HVAC)
- Real estate agencies
- Gyms & fitness studios
- Salons & beauty services
