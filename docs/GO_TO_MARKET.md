# MojuCrew — Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target Customer (MVP)

**Primary:** SMB owners in non-PII industries
- Restaurants, cafes, food & beverage
- Salons, barbershops, beauty services
- Gyms, fitness studios, personal trainers
- Tradespeople (plumbers, electricians, HVAC, cleaners)
- Real estate agents / small agencies
- Retail shops (local, e-commerce)

**Profile:**
- 1–20 employees
- Owner-operated
- Feels overwhelmed managing customer comms + reputation
- Not technical — needs plug-and-play
- Willing to pay $79–$350/mo if it saves them time

---

## Phase 1 — Validation (Months 1–2)

**Goal:** 10 paying beta customers

**Actions:**
- Manual outreach to local SMBs in target categories
- Offer free 30-day trial of MojuChat + MojuReviews
- Onboard personally, gather feedback
- Build case studies and before/after metrics
- Refine onboarding to under 30 minutes

**Channels:**
- Direct DMs on Instagram/Facebook to local businesses
- Local Facebook business groups
- Cold email to Google My Business listed SMBs
- Personal network referrals

---

## Phase 2 — Growth (Months 3–6)

**Goal:** 50–100 paying customers

**Actions:**
- Launch on Product Hunt
- Create short-form video content (TikTok/Reels) showing the bots in action
- SEO content targeting "AI chatbot for [restaurants/salons/gyms]"
- Partner with local business associations and chambers of commerce
- Launch affiliate/referral program ($50 per referral)

**Channels:**
- Organic social (behind-the-scenes, demos, SMB success stories)
- Google Ads targeting SMB owners searching for chat/CRM tools
- Facebook/Instagram ads to business owners

---

## Phase 3 — Scale (Months 6–12)

**Goal:** 500+ customers, agency channel launched

**Actions:**
- Launch white-label reseller program for digital agencies
- Integrate with popular SMB platforms (Shopify, Wix, Square)
- Expand to additional industries (avoiding PII-heavy ones for now)
- Build out MojuResearch and MojuContent bots
- Explore partnerships with business phone/VoIP providers

**Channels:**
- Agency partnerships (they resell to their clients)
- App marketplaces (Shopify App Store, Wix App Market)
- Outbound sales team

---

## Competitive Positioning

| vs. | MojuCrew Advantage |
|-----|--------------------|
| HubSpot | Simpler, cheaper, no learning curve |
| Intercom | SMB-focused pricing, bundled roles |
| Birdeye / Podium | Broader — reviews + chat + CRM in one |
| Zapier + AI | Pre-built roles, no setup required |
| Hiring staff | Fraction of the cost, always on |

**Tagline options:**
- "Your AI crew, ready to work."
- "Hire AI. Grow faster."
- "The team you can't afford not to have."

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Churn rate (target < 5%/mo)
- Time-to-value (how fast a new customer sees results)
- Bot resolution rate (% of chats handled without human)
- NPS / customer satisfaction
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) vs LTV

---

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| SMBs slow to adopt AI | Emphasize "hiring" framing, not tech |
| LLM costs eat margins | Use cheaper models for simple tasks, Claude for complex |
| Competition from big players | Stay niche, move fast, build community |
| High churn if bots underperform | Strong onboarding, human fallback option |
