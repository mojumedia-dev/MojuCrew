# MojuCrew — Platform Plan

## Vision

A unified AI workforce platform for SMBs. Each "crew member" is an AI bot that handles a specific business role — sold individually or as a bundle — making AI adoption feel like hiring staff, not buying software.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. MojuChat — AI Receptionist
- Embeds on the business website as a live chat widget
- Answers FAQs using the business's own knowledge base
- Books appointments (integrates with Calendly, Google Calendar)
- Qualifies and captures leads
- Escalates to a human when needed
- Supports after-hours responses

### 2. MojuCRM — AI CRM Manager
- Auto-logs all customer interactions (chat, email, form fills)
- Sends follow-up emails/SMS on behalf of the business
- Scores and prioritizes leads
- Tracks deal stages
- Integrates with HubSpot, Zoho, or operates as a lightweight standalone CRM
- Weekly pipeline summary reports

### 3. MojuReviews — AI Review Manager
- Monitors Google Business Profile, Yelp, Facebook reviews
- Auto-responds to reviews with tone-matched, personalized replies
- Flags negative reviews for human attention immediately
- Sends post-visit nudges to happy customers asking for a review
- Weekly reputation score dashboard

### 4. MojuResearch — AI Market Researcher
- Tracks competitor websites, social, and pricing changes
- Monitors industry news and relevant keywords
- Delivers weekly digest to the business owner
- Highlights actionable insights (e.g. "competitor X dropped prices 10%")
- Powered by web scraping + LLM summarization

### 5. MojuContent — AI Content & Social Bot
- Drafts social media posts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- Repurposes content across channels
- Responds to comments and DMs
- Schedules posts
- Keeps brand voice consistent using a saved style profile

1. **MojuChat** — highest immediate value, easiest to demo

2. **Non-technical setup** — onboard in under 30 minutes
3. **Role-framing** — marketed as "staff" not "software"
4. **SMB pricing** — affordable at every stage
5. **White-label ready** — agencies can resell under their own brand
